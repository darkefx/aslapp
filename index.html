How to use
Show your hand to the camera and hold an ASL sign.

Hold still for 2 seconds — the ring fills up and the letter commits automatically.

Confidence >80% triggers the hold timer. Lower confidence resets it.

Use Delete to remove the last letter, or Clear to start over.
